About Me

Hello! I’m Michael, the founder and driving force behind Mike's Delivery and Business LLC. My journey has been anything but conventional, weaving through various fields and experiences to where I am today.

Retail Beginnings

My professional story began in the retail sector. During this time, I honed my customer service skills and developed a keen understanding of consumer needs and business operations. This phase of my career laid a solid foundation of discipline, work ethic, and a customer-first mindset.

Transition to Delivery Services

Inspired by the growing gig economy, I transitioned into the delivery field, initially working as a DoorDash driver and Instacart shopper. These roles were pivotal, exposing me to the logistics and operational challenges of delivery services. I learned firsthand the importance of efficiency, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Entrepreneurial Leap

The experiences and insights gained from my delivery roles fueled my entrepreneurial spirit. I founded Mike's Delivery and Business LLC with the vision of providing innovative logistics and delivery solutions. Our custom delivery app has been a game-changer, enabling us to streamline operations and offer unparalleled service to our clients.

Specialized Services

While running my business full-time, I expanded my expertise into escorting oversize loads, such as mobile homes. This specialized service has allowed us to diversify and tackle more complex logistics challenges, further establishing our reputation in the industry.

Commitment to Excellence

Throughout my journey, my commitment to excellence has remained unwavering. I believe in continuous improvement, innovative thinking, and putting our clients’ needs at the forefront of everything we do.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about my journey. I look forward to connecting and exploring how we can work together to meet your logistics and business needs.
